Amias Hanley

    Bloom: We're Going to Die

    Single channel video, 2-channel sound, 12 minutes, 36 seconds

    Filmed in 2015 on the salt marshes of Wadawurrung Country, this work features a single continuous shot. It grapples with the dynamics of carbon-based life cycles—births, bonds, drifts, metabolisations—and the ephemerality and finitude expressed in these processes. The sound composition, created in 2020, was developed entirely on the Moog Mother 32 semi-modular analogue synthesiser.
