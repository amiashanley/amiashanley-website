Amias Hanley

    Resonant Incantations

    Single channel video, 2-channel sound, 7 minutes, 48 seconds

    Resonant Incantations is a collaborative work by Amias Hanley and Melody Woodnutt, commissioned by Speak Percussion in 2020 as part of their SD Series. The resulting work brings together two distinct recursive practices to explore themes of deep time, repetition, hauntology, generation, and the collapse and intensification of site, space, excess, and decay.

    Amias Hanley's compositions for the work were partially developed during their 2020 residency at the Bogong Centre for Sound Culture, located on Dhudhuroa and Jaitmatang Country. Melody Woodnutt's 16mm film component originated in 2018 during The Weight of Mountains film residency on the lands of the Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in.
