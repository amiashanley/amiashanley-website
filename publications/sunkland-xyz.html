Amias Hanley

    SUNKLAND.XYZ

    Hanley, A., & Hase, P. (2021). SUNKLAND.XYZ. Disclaimer Journal.

    Abstract

    SUNKLAND.XYZ is an interactive browser presentation of an audio work originally composed for 24.2 channels by Amias Hanley, and exhibited at McClelland Sculpture Park and Gallery by Liquid Architecture. The browser-based presentation of this work is a collaboration between Amias Hanley and Patrick Hase, who designed and implemented the custom movement and visualisation system.

    Set in a speculative swampland informed by the pre-colonised Carrum Carrum Swamp on Bunurong Country, the audio work explores the transformation of species, forms, and forces through open-ended vocal exchanges. The swampland is presented across five interconnected layers, each representing slices of tangled time—merging past remnants and future imaginings into a nonlinear present. Using a transparent sphere (the listener), participants can navigate these layers to explore the composition of sounds, textures, and spatial arrangements.

    SUNKLAND.XYZ reflects on queer kinship, hybridity, and care, asking how multispecies perspectives and sonic exchanges reshape relationships and decision-making. Through morphing vocal transitions, it imagines kinship as an emergent, shared process—an offering to listen, imagine, and reframe interspecies connections.

    Keywords

    Queer kinship, hybridity, multispecies ecologies, vocal transitions, sonic exchange, swamplands, water governance
