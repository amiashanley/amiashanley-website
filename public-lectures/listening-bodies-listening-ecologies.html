Amias Hanley

    Listening bodies, listening ecologies

    Artist talk, 90 minutes

    The fourth event in the Audition series, convened by Ben Byrne, featured collaborators Amias Hanley and Patrick Hase. The programme included Hase leading an artist talk and live Let’s Play of their collaborative interactive audio work SUNKLAND.XYZ (2021), alongside Hanley’s discussion and presentation of their multichannel audio works, Sonic Entanglements: Bodies of the Valley (2018) and SUNKLAND (2021).

    Audition was a five-part series for sonic research and practice, developed by researchers and artists at three RMIT University labs: Sound Art & Auditory Culture, Screen and Sound Cultures, and Design & Sonic Practice. Each event was convened by a different group member, exploring diverse approaches to sonic inquiry across Melbourne Naarm, AU
